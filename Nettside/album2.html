FREEFALL

The album FREEFALL takes listeners on a haunting journey through the depths of the human experience. With a sonic landscape drenched in dark tones, each track invites introspection and contemplation.

  • 3 AM - This opener sets a restless mood, exploring the thoughts that surface in the stillness of the night. Its eerie melodies and driving beats create a sense of urgency and vulnerability.
  • Dear Nightmare - A poignant letter to one's fears, this track blends haunting lyrics with a moody atmosphere. It navigates the struggle between confronting inner demons and succumbing to them.
  • Overflow - As emotions spill over, this piece captures the essence of overwhelming feelings. Its layered instrumentation mirrors the chaos of thoughts and sensations that can consume us.
  • [BLANK] - An introspective interlude that offers a moment of silence in the chaos. This track allows listeners to pause and reflect, emphasizing the theme of searching for meaning amidst uncertainty.

Together, these songs weave a powerful narrative about life's complexities, making FREEFALL a thought-provoking listening experience.
